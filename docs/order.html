💡 How Billing Works

Everything that affects your final bill — in plain English.

🚴 Delivery Charge
Always free: Bhosale Nagar · Triveni Nagar · Self Pickup
₹10 per meal for all other areas — unless your day total qualifies for free delivery (see below).
🎁 When Delivery Becomes Free
1 meal that day → free delivery if food total ≥ ₹100
2 or more meals that day → free delivery if combined food total ≥ ₹150

💡 Example: You order Breakfast (₹80) and pay ₹10 delivery. Later that day you add Dinner (₹90). Combined = ₹170 ≥ ₹150 — delivery becomes free, and the ₹10 you already paid is credited back on your next order that day.
🛒 Small Order Fee
An extra ₹10 is added if your Lunch or Dinner subtotal is below ₹50.
This does not apply to Breakfast, free delivery areas, or Pickup orders.
It is also waived once your day total crosses the free delivery threshold.
🏷️ Discounts (Auto-applied)
5% off when your total food spend for the day ≥ ₹300
7.5% off when your total food spend for the day ≥ ₹450

This is based on your entire day's total — so if you order multiple meals across the day and cross ₹300, the discount applies to everything placed that day (including earlier orders).

💡 Example: You order Breakfast ₹150 + Lunch ₹170 = ₹320 combined → 5% discount applied to the whole day automatically.
📈 Market Surcharge
A small surcharge of ₹1 for every ₹20 of food (≈5%) is added per meal to help us manage rising ingredient costs.

Examples:
₹40 meal → ₹2 surcharge
₹100 meal → ₹5 surcharge
₹150 meal → ₹8 surcharge
🔥 6th Day Loyalty Reward
Order on 5 consecutive days → your 6th consecutive day is surcharge-free.
All market surcharges from the previous 5 days are also credited back on that 6th day.

💡 Sundays don't count — since we're closed on Sundays, the streak continues through the weekend. Saturday + Monday = consecutive days. This resets every 6 days — keep ordering to keep earning!
⭐ Google Review Reward
Leave us a 5-star Google review and get 10% off on your next 3 meal orders.
This is verified manually by our team. Once confirmed, the discount is applied automatically on checkout.
